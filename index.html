Welcome to the Organizational AI Risk Disclosure Explorer

This tool maps what large global companies actually say about AI risk. We reviewed public disclosures from roughly 200 of the world's largest companies and coded every risk they acknowledge against the AI Risk Repository taxonomy.

Filter by company, sector, actor role, risk, or mitigation, and compare what companies disclose against expert-rated severity, sector vulnerability, and actor responsibility.

⚠This is a research prototype. The data and analyses are preliminary and not yet validated.
